About Me

Hello! I'm Kyle, the instructor behind Katzhu Piano Studio. With over a decade of experience in music and a passion for education, I specialize in teaching students aged 6 to 25 in a way that’s engaging, structured, and confidence-building.

I believe every student learns differently, and I tailor my lessons to support individual goals—whether it’s mastering classical repertoire, preparing for recitals or exams, or simply enjoying the process of making music. My studio is a place for creativity, curiosity, and growth.

When I’m not teaching, I’m composing and producing music, which brings a contemporary edge to my approach. I bring that same energy and modern understanding into the classroom to help young musicians thrive.
